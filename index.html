F-35B Lift System Simulation

Explore an interactive 3D simulation of the F-35B Lightning II's revolutionary Short Take-Off and Vertical Landing (STOVL) lift system. This WebGL experience demonstrates the complex engineering behind the aircraft's unique propulsion configuration.

Features:

  • Interactive 3D visualization of the lift system components
  • Real-time manipulation and exploration
  • Detailed view of the thrust vectoring nozzle and lift fan
